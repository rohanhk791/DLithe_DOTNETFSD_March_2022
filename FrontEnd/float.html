Farmers

A person who pays a fixed sum for some privilege or source of income. A person who cultivates land or crops or raises animals. A Farmer manages farms, ranches, greenhouses, nurseries, and other agricultural production organizations. Farmers are involved in planting, cultivating, performing post-harvest duties, overseeing livestock, and supervising farm labor depending on the type of farm.
